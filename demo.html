How to Use Google Fonts with Your Specimen

Step 1: Go to Google Fonts

Visit fonts.google.com and browse fonts

Step 2: Select a Font

Click on a font you like, then click "Select" for the weights you want

Step 3: Copy the URL

In the "Use on the web" section, copy the entire URL from the <link> tag

Example URLs you can copy:
https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Inter:wght@300;400;500;600;700&display=swap
https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Roboto:wght@300;400;500;700&display=swap
https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Poppins:wght@300;400;500;600;700&display=swap

Step 4: Load in Specimen

Paste the URL into the "Google Font URL" field in your specimen page and click "Load Font"
